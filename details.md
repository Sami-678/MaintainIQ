---------------------------Complete Process------------------------------

1. ------------------- ROLES-----------------------

. Is project mein total 3 main roles hain
  1. Administrator 
  2. Technician 
  3. User
 
------------------- ROLES-----------------------



2. ------------------WHAT TASK THEY PERFORM--------------------

. Administrator : Assets ko register aur manage karta hai, 
                  technicians ko kaam assign karta hai, aur pooray system ki settings handle karta hai.

.Technician: Apne assigned kaam ko dekhta hai, inspection 
             karta hai, aur kharab cheezon ko theek kar ke issue resolve karta hai.

. Public User: QR code scan kar ke asset ka page dekhta hai 
               aur wahan kisi bhi kharabi ya issue ko report karta hai


------------------WHAT TASK THEY PERFORM--------------------



3. -----------------WHAT TYPE OF ACCESS THEY HAVE---------------

. Administrator : Full system access (Internal Dashboard, 
                  analytics, settings, asset data editing, aur user management)

. Technician: Restricted internal access (Sirf apne assigned 
              issues dekhne, maintenance notes dalnay, aur status update karne ka access).

. Public User: Public-facing access (Bina login kiye sirf 
               safe/public asset details dekhne aur issue form submit karne ka access).


-----------------WHAT TYPE OF ACCESS THEY HAVE---------------


4. ---------------WHAT TYPE OF WORK THEY HAVE TO PERFORM------


. Administrator: Management aur assignment ka work (Data 
                 handling aur team monitoring).
.  Technician: Fieldwork aur technical resolution 
               (Inspections, parts replacement entry, costs record karna, aur proof upload karna).  
               
. Public User: Data entry aur reporting (Natural language 
               mein complaint darj karna).  


---------------WHAT TYPE OF WORK THEY HAVE TO PERFORM------

5. ------------------WORK FLOW------------------

. Admin kisi asset (jaise classroom projector) ko register
  karega, jisse ek unique QR code generate hoga.  

. Koi bhi public user us QR code ko scan kar ke 
  mobile-friendly page par jayega aur kharabi report karega.  

. AI Feature us complaint ko samajh kar ek professional 
  title, category, aur initial checks suggest karega, jise user review kar ke submit kar dega.  

. Admin internal dashboard par yeh issue dekh kar Technician
  ko assign karega.  

. Technician kaam khatam kar ke maintenance notes dalay ka
  aur status ko 'Resolved' kar dega.
